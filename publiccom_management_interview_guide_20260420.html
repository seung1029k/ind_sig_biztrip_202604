Restricted DD Tool

Public.com Management Interview Guide

Founder, CFO, GC, CTO, Growth, HR 인터뷰에서 valuation과 deal certainty를 바꾸는 질문만 남겼습니다. 발표를 듣는 미팅이 아니라 핵심 가정을 깨는 미팅이어야 합니다.

Founders / Co-CEO

Strategy
  1. Why sell now instead of staying independent for another 24 months?
  2. What are the three biggest constraints today: capital, compliance, product, distribution, or hiring?
  3. Which decisions must remain autonomous post-acquisition?
  4. If Mirae owns the company, what exactly gets faster and what gets slower?
  5. What would materially break if one founder leaves within 6 months?
  6. Which KPI do you trust most and which KPI do you think investors misunderstand?

CFO / Finance Lead

Numbers
  1. Reconcile internal 2025 revenue to any external revenue narratives used in fundraising or advisory materials.
  2. What is normalized 2026 run-rate revenue under lower rates and lower crypto activity?
  3. Which cost items are truly structural versus one-time?
  4. What minimum capital buffer do you believe is prudent post-close?
  5. What are the top three reasons gross margin could compress?
  6. What does “core brokerage profitable” mean in accounting terms?

General Counsel / Compliance

Regulatory
  1. Walk us through the exact root cause of each FINRA matter.
  2. What remediation is complete, partially complete, or still behaviorally fragile?
  3. Any open exam comments, informal regulator feedback, or threatened matters?
  4. Which area of the 1017 review is most likely to attract scrutiny?
  5. What books-and-records, marketing, or best-execution processes remain manual?
  6. Where would you add senior compliance talent immediately if budget were available?

Operations / Product / People

Execution
COO / Operations
  1. What is the highest-risk operational process today?
  2. How dependent are you on Apex, Zero Hash, and other critical partners?
  3. What would happen operationally if a key vendor changed pricing or paused onboarding?
  4. Where do customer complaints concentrate?
CTO / Product / AI
  1. Which AI capabilities are proprietary and which are orchestration around third-party models?
  2. How does AI usage change deposits, engagement, AUM retention, or conversion?
  3. What is the monetization path for Generated Assets today?
  4. What are the main failure modes: hallucination, latency, stale data, compliance review, or explainability?
Head of Growth / Marketing
  1. What share of funded account growth is promo-driven versus product-driven?
  2. What is true CAC by channel after transfer incentives and paid media?
  3. What happened in the UK, and what did you learn from that failure?
  4. Which acquired customer books have performed best and worst?
Head of People / HR
  1. Who are the 20 people we cannot afford to lose?
  2. What retention package actually changes behavior for founders and top engineering talent?
  3. What culture risks do you see under a larger parent company?
  4. What unresolved scars remain from prior RIFs?

Closing Prompts

  3. If you were in our seat, what would you diligence hardest in the next two weeks?
